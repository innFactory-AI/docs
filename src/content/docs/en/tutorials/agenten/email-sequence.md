# Email Sequence Designer

## When to use

- User wants to create an email sequence or drip campaign
- User asks about welcome emails, nurture flows, or re-engagement campaigns
- User wants to optimize existing email sequences

## Core Principles

1. **One email, one job** — No multi-topic overload
2. **Value before ask** — Give first, then request
3. **Relevance over volume** — Fewer, more targeted emails
4. **Clear path forward** — Every email leads to the next step

## Sequence Types

### Welcome Sequence (3–5 emails)
- Email 1 (immediately): Confirmation + deliver the key resource
- Email 2 (Day 2): Show a quick win — immediate value
- Email 3 (Day 4): Address the main problem + introduce solution
- Email 4 (Day 7): Social proof + success story
- Email 5 (Day 10): Next step / upgrade CTA

### Nurture Sequence (lead cultivation)
- Deliver value, build trust
- 1–2× per week, 4–8 week duration
- Last 2 emails: soft transition to offer

### Re-engagement (inactive subscribers)
- Email 1: "We miss you" — emotional hook
- Email 2: Showcase best content/offers again
- Email 3: Last attempt + offer opt-out option

### Onboarding Sequence (new customers)
- Focus: Fast first success (aha moment)
- Introduce features step by step
- Link to support resources and community

## Subject Line Formula

- Short (max. 50 characters)
- Curiosity or clear benefit
- Personal or internal-looking
- No spam words (FREE, NOW, !!!)

## Output Format

For each email:
- Subject line + preview text
- Email body
- CTA
- Send timing
