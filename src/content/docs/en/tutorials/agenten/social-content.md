```markdown

# Social Content Creator

## When to use

- User wants to write or schedule social media posts
- User is looking for a social media content strategy
- User wants to repurpose content across platforms

## Platform Quick Reference

| Platform | Best content | Optimal frequency | Tone |
|----------|-------------|------------------|------|
| LinkedIn | Professional insights, stories | 3–5×/week | Professional, personal |
| Twitter/X | Opinions, threads, reactions | 1–3×/day | Direct, concise |
| Instagram | Visual, behind-the-scenes | 4–7×/week | Inspiring, authentic |
| TikTok | Entertainment, education, trends | 1×/day | Casual, energetic |

## Content Pillars Framework

Define 3–5 core topics (pillars) that fit the brand and expertise:

Example for SaaS founder:
1. Product updates & features
2. Behind the scenes (building in public)
3. Industry insights & opinions
4. Customer successes
5. Personal learnings

## Hook Formulas

- **Curiosity**: "What if...?", "Most people don't know that..."
- **Story**: "6 months ago I was..."
- **Value**: "5 things I learned about X"
- **Contrarian**: "I disagree with everyone on X"

## Content Repurposing System

From one blog post, create:
- 1 LinkedIn long-form post (key takeaways)
- 3–5 Twitter/X posts (one point each)
- 1 Instagram carousel (visual breakdown)
- 1 TikTok script (casual explanation)

## Engagement Strategy

- Daily 10–15 minutes: Reply to comments
- Leave quality comments on others' posts
- Build relationships with other creators

## Output Format

1. **Ready-to-publish posts** for requested platform(s)
2. **Content calendar** for the next 1–2 weeks
3. **Variants** for A/B testing hooks
```
