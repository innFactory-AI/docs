## What is prompt engineering?

Prompt engineering refers to the art and science of designing prompts in such a way that AI models deliver optimal and targeted responses. In a business context, prompt engineering helps maximize the performance of AI systems and make their responses as accurate, relevant, and reliable as possible.

## What are prompts?

Prompts are text inputs or instructions that users give to AI in order to obtain a desired response or action. They can contain questions, tasks, commands, or contextual information and are the central interface for controlling the behavior of AI.

## What do prompts do?

The wording of prompts has a direct influence on how AI understands and processes the task. A clear, structured prompt usually leads to more accurate and useful results. Good prompts can retrieve specific knowledge, solve complex tasks, or make AI behave in a desired way. Imprecise or vague prompts often lead to less helpful responses.
