title: Fileforge
description: Fileforge add-on
---

With the Fileforge add-on, you can generate complex files from simple prompts and raw data.

**Comprehensive document creation**: Generate native Word (.docx), Excel (.xlsx), PowerPoint (.pptx) and PDF files. Direct conversion of Markdown text into finished Office documents or PDFs also works seamlessly.

**Data processing & code export**: Convert structured data such as JSON or CSV directly into clean Excel spreadsheets or Word documents. You can also generate any text-based files (SQL, XML, HTML, as well as code scripts such as Python or JS).

**Visual Charts**: Visualise raw numbers directly as finished PNG charts (bar, line, pie charts, etc.).

**Intelligent template management**: Upload your own global templates (DOCX, XLSX, POTX). CompanyGPT can populate these templates with your current data on demand (e.g. dynamically replace placeholders in Word documents).
